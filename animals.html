Ape

It is a large primate that lacks a tail, including the gorilla, chimpanzees, orangutan, and gibbons.

Dog

It is a domesticated carnivorous mammal that typically has a long snout, an acute sense of smell, nonretractable claws, and a barking, howling, or whining voice.

Jaguar

It is a large heavily built cat that has a yellowish-brown coat with black spots, found mainly in the dense forests of Central and South America.

Mouse

A mouse is a small rodent. Characteristically, mice are known to have a pointed snout, small rounded ears, a body-length scaly tail, and a high breeding rate.
